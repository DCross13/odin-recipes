Special K Bars Recipe

Description

This is the best recipe for an easy dessert - my grandmother's are always a hit.

Ingredients

  • 6 oz. chocolate chips
  • 6 oz. butterscotch chips
  • 1 c peanut butter
  • 1 c white sugar
  • 1 c light corn syrup
  • 5 c Special K cereal, crushed (can use gluten-free)

Steps

  1. In a large pot over medium heat, combine the corn syrup and the sugar until small bubbles form around the edge of the pan. Do NOT boil.
  2. Once bubbles form, remove from heat and add peanut butter - mix thoroughly.
  3. After mixture is homogenous, add in crushed cereal and continue mixing.
  4. Spread cereal mixture into a 9x13 pan and allow to cool.
  5. Meanwhile, use a double boiler to melt chocolate chips and butterscotch chips together.
  6. Pour chocolate mixture over cearl/peanut butter mixture.
  7. Refrigerate 1-2 hours before serving.
