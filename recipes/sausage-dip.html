Sausage Dip Recipe

Description

This is the best recipe for a gameday snack - most ingredients are already on hand!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs. of hot Italian sausage
  • 2 packages of cream cheese
  • 2 cans of Rotel
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. In a large pot, brown the Italian sausage. Try to get it cooked through, there's not much idle time for it to cook later.
  2. Once the sausage is cooked, add the cream cheese. Break it up with your spatula and mix it until the cream cheese is mostly liquified.
  3. Drain the water from the Rotel, and add both cans to the pot - stir well.
  4. Once the dip is thoroughly mixed together, turn the heat to low and serve with tortilla chips.
