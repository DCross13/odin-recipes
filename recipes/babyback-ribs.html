Babyback Ribs Recipe

Description

This is the best recipe for smokey babybacks - all you need is a smoker and 6 hours of patience.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 racks of babyback ribs (silverskin removed)
  • Yellow mustard
  • Rib rub (I prefer Four Brothers, use your favorite)
  • BBQ sauce (I like a Carolina-style sauce, but this is up to you)
  • Apple juice
  • Brown sugar
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Honey

Steps

  1. The night before you plan to cook/eat, thouroughly coat the ribs with mustard.
  2. Apply your chosen rib rub, ensuring that every square inch of meat and bone is coated.
  3. Follow the 3-2-1 method, detailed here.
  4. Once the ribs are cooked to 203 *F, apply your chosen BBQ sauce and return to the smoker for 10-15 minutes or until sauce is tacky.
